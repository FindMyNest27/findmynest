🔒 Privacy
Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

What information we collect

We collect personal information from you, including information about your:

  • Name
  • Contact information
  • Location
  • Computer or network
  • Interactions with us
  • Billing or purchase information

Why we collect it

We collect your personal information in order to:

  • Operate the platform — to provide services such as listing properties, processing enquiries and connecting landlords with renters.
  • Trust and safety — maintaining a secure marketplace by monitoring for misuse or fraudulent behaviour.
  • Personalisation and marketing — delivering relevant content and tailored listings based on your preferences and browsing behaviour.
  • User communication — sending service updates and relevant communications, which you can manage via your account settings.

Providing some information is optional. If you choose not to enter any such information, we will be unable to perform some of our services.

Your rights

You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong. If you'd like to ask for a copy of your information, or to have it corrected, please contact us at hello@findmynest.co.nz
