📖 For Renters
Renting Guide

New to renting in New Zealand? Here's everything you need to know to find your perfect nest.

🔍 Searching for a Property
Use our search to filter by location, price, bedrooms and property type. Save listings you love and come back to them any time.

📝 Applying for a Rental
When you find a property you love, contact the landlord directly through FindMyNest. Have your ID, proof of income and references ready to go.

📋 Your Rights as a Tenant
Under the Residential Tenancies Act, you have strong rights as a renter in NZ. Landlords must provide a healthy, safe home that meets Healthy Homes Standards.

💰 Bond & Rent
Bond is capped at 4 weeks rent and must be lodged with Tenancy Services. Rent can only be increased once every 12 months with 60 days notice.

For official tenancy information visit tenancy.govt.nz

🏠 For Landlords
Landlord Guide

Everything you need to know to list your property and find great tenants on FindMyNest.

📸 Creating a Great Listing
Good photos make all the difference. Use natural light, tidy the space first, and take photos of every room. Listings with 6+ photos get significantly more enquiries.

💲 Setting the Right Rent
Research similar properties in your area before setting your price. Overpriced listings stay vacant longer — a competitive rent means faster tenants.

✅ Healthy Homes Standards
All rentals in NZ must comply with Healthy Homes Standards covering heating, insulation, ventilation, moisture and drainage. Non-compliance can result in fines up to $7,200.

📋 Tenancy Agreements
Always have a written tenancy agreement. You can use the standard Tenancy Services agreement or a solicitor-prepared one.

For official landlord information visit tenancy.govt.nz
